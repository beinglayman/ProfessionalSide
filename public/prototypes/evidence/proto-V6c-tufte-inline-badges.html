Situation

Our authentication system relied on a legacy session-based approach that couldn't scale across our growing microservice architecture. Three critical services — user-auth, payment-gateway, and notification-hub — each maintained separate session stores, causing inconsistent login states and a 12% session-drop rate.

Task

I was tasked with migrating all three services to OAuth2 with JWT tokens, establishing a centralized identity provider, and ensuring zero-downtime during the transition. The migration needed to complete within Q1 2026 while maintaining backward compatibility.
